My Values and Beliefs

Integrity is the foundation of all my professional and personal interactions. I believe in being honest, transparent, and ethical in every decision I make. Continuous learning is a core value – the tech field evolves rapidly, and I commit to lifelong learning to stay relevant and innovative. Collaboration is key to success; I value diverse perspectives and believe that collective problem-solving yields better results than working in isolation.

I also believe in giving back to the community. As I grow in my career, I aim to mentor others and contribute to open-source projects, ensuring that knowledge and opportunities are shared equitably. Finally, I strive for balance – between ambition and well-being, between work and life, to maintain sustainability in my professional journey.

My Career Aspirations

My long-term career goal is to become a senior AI engineer specializing in computer vision and natural language processing. I aim to work at the intersection of research and industry, translating cutting-edge academic research into practical, real-world applications that solve meaningful problems.

In the short term, I plan to pursue a master's degree in Computer Science (Machine Learning track) to deepen my technical expertise. I hope to secure a research assistant position during my studies to gain hands-on experience with advanced AI models and contribute to impactful research projects.

After completing my master's, I aim to join a tech company or research lab where I can work on building scalable AI systems. Ultimately, I want to lead a team of engineers and researchers, driving innovation in AI while fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment.

My Interests and Hobbies

Beyond academics and professional development, I have a diverse range of interests that keep me balanced and inspired. I am an avid hiker and outdoor enthusiast – exploring nature helps me clear my mind and gain perspective on complex problems.

I also enjoy photography, particularly landscape and street photography. This hobby has taught me to see beauty in small details and has improved my visual thinking, which translates well to my work in computer vision. In my free time, I play the guitar and enjoy music production – a creative outlet that complements my analytical work in tech.
