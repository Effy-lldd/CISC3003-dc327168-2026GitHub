    • My Values and Beliefs

    Integrity is the foundation of all my professional and personal interactions. I believe in being honest, transparent, and ethical in every decision I make. Continuous learning is a core value – the tech field evolves rapidly, and I commit to lifelong learning to stay relevant and innovative.

    Collaboration is key to success; I value diverse perspectives and believe that collective problem-solving yields better results than working in isolation.

    My Career Aspirations

    My long-term career goal is to become a senior AI engineer specializing in NLP. I aim to work at the intersection of research and industry, translating academic research into real-world applications.

    In the short term, I plan to pursue a master's degree in Computer Science to deepen my technical expertise.

    My Interests and Hobbies

    Beyond academics and professional development, I have a diverse range of interests that keep me balanced and inspired. I am an avid hiker and outdoor enthusiast – exploring nature helps me clear my mind and gain perspective on complex problems.


    I also enjoy photography, particularly landscape and street photography. This hobby has taught me to see beauty in small details and has improved my visual thinking, which translates well to my work in computer vision. In my free time, I play the guitar and enjoy music production – a creative outlet that complements my analytical work in tech.
