Reflection & Future Directions

"Learning is a continuous process of applying basic skills to real-world problems."

Personal Reflection on Learning Journey

The CISC3003 Web Programming course has helped me build basic web development skills, which complement my Computer Science foundation. Through this course, I learned the importance of attention to detail and practical application of technical knowledge – key skills for IT support work.

My part-time research and internship experiences have strengthened my data processing and problem-solving abilities, which I believe are valuable for IT support roles. I have also learned that continuous learning is essential in the tech field, and I will keep improving my practical skills to meet the requirements of IT support work.

Looking ahead, I will focus on applying my technical skills (programming, data analysis, web development basics) to IT support work. I plan to gain practical experience through internships and entry-level positions, and continuously learn new skills to adapt to the needs of the IT support field. My goal is to become a competent IT Support Technician who can effectively solve technical problems and provide reliable technical support.
